Withdrawal Policy

Virtual Currency Disclaimer

Coins used in the ApnaJeet app are virtual points. They do not represent real money or legal tender. Coins can only be used within the platform as per applicable rules.

1. Eligibility

  • Minimum withdrawal: 40 coins = ₹20
  • User account must be verified
  • No active policy violations

2. Coin Earning Sources

  • Skill-based tournaments
  • Rewarded advertisements

3. Withdrawal Methods

  • UPI transfer
  • OR redeem code sent to registered email

4. Processing

All withdrawal requests are manually reviewed by the admin team.
Standard processing time: 24–72 hours.

Processing time may increase during high traffic periods or additional verification requirements.

5. Fraud Prevention

Suspicious activity, multiple accounts, device mismatch, or policy violations may result in withdrawal rejection.

Admin approval is mandatory and final.
