Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled on the ApnaJeet platform.

1. No Refund Policy

  • Coins once used to join a tournament are non-refundable
  • Tournament entry fees are paid using virtual coins, not cash
  • No refunds are provided for ongoing or completed tournaments

2. Tournament Cancellation

In the event of a tournament cancellation by ApnaJeet, coins may be refunded at the sole discretion of the admin.

3. Transparency

This refund policy exists to ensure transparency, fair competition, and to prevent misuse of the platform.
